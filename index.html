About Me

I'm a Test Analyst, who has an keen interest in test automation.

My name is Marije originally from The Netherlands, now living in Brisbane.

I currently work as a Test Analyst. I have experience in various domains during my career, including government, insurance, mining and banking.

I've had exposure to test automation on a project at the Department of Agriculture and Fisheries where a basic regression test suite was delivered to reduce the manual testing effort. This piqued my interest to learn more about "Automation in Test" a term used by Richard Bradshaw. Not just the coding side of things but also the use of automation, and how it can support testing efforts.

People like Richard Bradshaw, Angie Jones and Bas Dijkstra are people I follow. They've done great work in improving the view and expectations of test automation.

As a team player, I work closely with product managers, designers and software engineers to ensure that we as a team are delivering an unique experience to users.
I believe quality is something that should be checked throughout the process, not just at the end.

I am always happy to assist and will take ownership of problems, actively seeking solutions until an agreed resolution has been achieved.

I can tell you more about me when we meet for coffee!
